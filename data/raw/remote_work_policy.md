## **Remote Work Policy** 

## **Version 1.0** 

Effective Date: January 1, 2026 Owner: Human Resources 

## **1. Purpose** 

This policy establishes the organization's expectations and procedures. It is intended to provide employees and managers with clear guidance and ensure consistent decision-making across the company. Employees are expected to read, understand, and comply with this policy. Failure to follow these requirements may result in corrective action. This section should be interpreted together with applicable employment laws and internal procedures. 

The company periodically reviews this policy and may revise it when business needs, legal requirements, or operational practices change. Where applicable, department-specific procedures may supplement this policy. The Human Resources department is responsible for maintaining the official version.
